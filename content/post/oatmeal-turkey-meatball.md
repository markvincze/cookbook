# Ingredients

 - 500g ground meat (turkey preferred)
 - 3 tbsp rolled oats
 - 1 small onion
 - 2 cloves garlic
 - 1 egg
 - 1 tbsp paprika
 - salt
 - black pepper
 - oil

# Recipe

Finely chop the onion, fry it in a pan with the garlic.
Mix together the meat, egg, oats, onions, salt, pepper, paprika, let it rest for 30 minutes.
Preheat the oven to 180 C.
With wet hand form the mixture into balls, align on baking sheet and brush with a little oil.
Bake for 30 minutes.
