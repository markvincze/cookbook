# Ingredients

 - 400g green beans
 - 2 tsp oil
 - 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
 - 1 tbsp cooking wine
 - 2 tsp minced garlic
 - 2 tsp grated ginger
 - 1/2 tsp sugar
 - 1/2 tsp sesame oil
 - 1/4 tsp salt
 - 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
 - Garnish: sesame seeds

# Recipe

Combine sauce ingredients
Dry-fry beans in a little oil for 10 minutes, until tender crisp and charred.
Pour sauce into skillet, sautÃ© for a couple of minutes until thickened and the beans are coated.
Top with sesame seeds.
