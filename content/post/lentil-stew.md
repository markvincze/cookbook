# Ingredients

250g lentils
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
1 tbsp oil
1 tsp paprika
1l water
2 bay leaves
salt
200ml sour cream
1 tbsp flour
1 tbsp mustard

# Recipe

Wash lentils, soak in water for 1-2 hours.
Finely chop the onion, fry in a pot, add garlic.
Add paprika, and pour in the water.
Drain the lentils and add them to the pot. Add water to cover the lentil.
Simmer until lentils are softened.
Mix the sour cream with the flour, temper it with the hot cooking water, then add to the stew.
Add the mustard, and season with salt and pepper.
