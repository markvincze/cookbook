Steak

Mar 20, 2021
  1. Season steak with salt and pepper, and let it sit at room temp for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 110 C, and bake the steak for around 45 minutes, or until the middle registers at 50 C.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil on high heat, and sear the steak on both sides for 2 minutes.
  4. Add 2-3 tbsp butter, 1 clove of garlic and a sprig of rosemary, and baste both sides for 90 seconds.
  5. The internal temperature of the steak should be 60 C.
  6. Sear the sides of the steak too for 60 seconds.
  7. Let it rest for 5 minutes under aluminum foil before cutting it up.
