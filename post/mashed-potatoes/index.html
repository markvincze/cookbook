Mashed Potatoes

Mar 20, 2021
  1. Cook the peeled, chopped potatoes (1.5kg) in salty water, 15-20 minutes.
  2. Mash the potatoes.
  3. Melt ~100g butter. Stir it into the potatoes.
  4. Heat up 1 cup of cream, and add aromatics (2-3 cloves of garlic)
  5. Stir the cream into the the potatoes in small batches.
