Rice pudding

Dec 30, 2025

Ingredients

  • 150g rice
  • 50g butter
  • 50ml tej
  • salt
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar

Recipe

Start cooking rice in 200ml water. When all water is soaked in, mix in butter, then add the milk.

Add 1 pinch of salt, and cook until all the milk is soaked in. Mix in the sugar.

Serve with cocoa powder, cinnamon or jam.
