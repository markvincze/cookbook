Garlic knots

Oct 19, 2021

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 7/8 cup (207ml) warm water (105°F to 115°F)
  • 2 1/4 cups (300g) bread flour, plus more for flouring the work surface and your hands
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil, plus more for greasing the bowl and brushing the knots

Garlic butter

5 tablespoons unsalted butter 4 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 cup parsley, minced 1 teaspoon salt

Recipe

  1. Mix and knead the dough for 10 minutes
  2. Let it rise either at room temperature or overnight, until doubled in size
  3. Cut into 8 pieces, form into tubes, and tie into knots
  4. Brush with olive oil, cover the knots and let them rise until doubled, between 90-180 minutes
  5. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees, bake the knots until browned, 12-15 minutes
  6. Make the garlic butter by melting it, and cooking the garlic for 1-2 minutes, and adding the salt
  7. Take out the knots, let them cool for 5 minutes, and brush them with the garlic butter
