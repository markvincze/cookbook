Strudel

Oct 15, 2021

Crust (for 2 strudels)

  • 250g butter
  • 300g sugar
  • Squeeze of one lemon
  • One vanilla bean
  • 500g flour (00 or AP)
  • 1g salt
  • 6g baking powder
  • 40ml milk
  • 100g eggs

Filling (for 1 strudel)

  • 600g apples
  • 50g sugar
  • 50g toasted breadcrumbs
  • Little butter
  • 20g pine nuts
  • Little cinnamon
  • 25ml lemon juice

Crust is resting 30-60 minutes. Don’t have to knead a lot, knead a little before stretching.
